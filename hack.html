Key Details

Team Registration
  • Teams must have a minimum of 4 people
  • Teams register by 23:59 on Friday March 6th
  • Submit your team via the link that will be shared on Discord
⚠ Friday Evening — Don't Miss It

The event begins on the 3rd Floor of MIT Media Lab. After 9 PM we move to the Hackathon space on the 6th Floor.

  • All event info and rules are covered
  • Equipment and kits handed out
  • Teams are formed
  • Sponsor workshops begin
Saturday & Sunday
  • All events take place on the 6th Floor of MIT Media Lab
  • Hacks must be finished by 2:59 PM Sunday — judging starts at 3:00 PM sharp
  • Each team gets 2 minutes to present

What to Bring

Bring your laptop, chargers, and any personal tools. Each team gets a Hacker Bag with core components, plus access to our Parts Bank, 3D printers, and fab labs.
